Clean Oceans Campaign

Launched in 2018, the Clean Oceans Campaign is one of Clean Earth's largest and most impactful initiatives. The campaign aims to protect marine life by combating plastic pollution and other harmful waste that threatens the health of our oceans.

Key objectives of the Clean Oceans Campaign include:

  • Reducing plastic waste in oceans and coastal areas.
  • Collaborating with local communities and governments to establish sustainable waste management practices.
  • Raising awareness on the importance of ocean conservation through public outreach and education programs.
  • Supporting and promoting eco-friendly alternatives to plastic products.
  • Organizing beach cleanups and marine conservation projects.

Since its launch, the Clean Oceans Campaign has removed over 10,000 tons of plastic waste from coastlines and continues to grow through global partnerships and local community engagement.
