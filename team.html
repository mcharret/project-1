Meet Our Team

Leaders committed to the fight for environmental protection.

Greta Thunberg

Climate Activist

Known for her strong stance on climate change, Greta has inspired millions of young people worldwide to take action against global warming.

Paul Watson

Founder of Sea Shepherd

Paul is a veteran environmental activist, renowned for his direct actions to protect marine life and oceans through Sea Shepherd's global efforts.

Anne Hidalgo

Mayor of Paris

Anne is committed to implementing eco-friendly policies in Paris, transforming the city into a greener, more sustainable urban environment.

Pamela Anderson

Animal Rights Advocate
