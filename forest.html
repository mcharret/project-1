Save the Forests Campaign

The Save the Forests Campaign is one of Clean Earth's most essential initiatives aimed at preserving the worldâ€™s forests and combating deforestation. This campaign is focused on protecting the lungs of our planet, ensuring that forests continue to thrive and support biodiversity.

Key objectives of the Save the Forests Campaign include:

  • Fighting deforestation and illegal logging.
  • Collaborating with local governments and communities to promote sustainable forest management.
  • Reforestation and afforestation programs to restore degraded forest areas.
  • Raising awareness about the importance of forests in mitigating climate change.
  • Supporting indigenous communities that rely on forests for their livelihoods.

Since the campaign's inception, Clean Earth has planted over 5 million trees and restored more than 50,000 hectares of forest land. Our efforts continue to grow through partnerships with governments, NGOs, and local communities worldwide.
