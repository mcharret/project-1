Our Mission

At Clean Earth, our mission is to drive impactful change by advocating for environmental sustainability, protecting natural resources, and raising awareness about climate change.

  • Educating communities on eco-friendly practices and reducing carbon footprints.
  • Supporting conservation efforts to preserve biodiversity and ecosystems.
  • Promoting global cooperation to fight climate change and achieve environmental justice.
  • Implementing solutions for clean energy, waste management, and pollution reduction.

We believe in building a sustainable future where everyone is empowered to contribute to environmental preservation, ensuring a greener world for generations to come.

Our History

Founded in 2005, Clean Earth started as a grassroots movement driven by a group of concerned citizens who were passionate about protecting the environment. Over the years, we have grown into an international NGO with a powerful voice in the global environmental movement.

Some of our key milestones include:

  • 2007: Launching our first major campaign to reduce single-use plastic in coastal areas.
  • 2010: Partnering with international organizations to promote sustainable energy projects in developing countries.
  • 2015: Playing a key role in advocating for stricter climate targets during the Paris Agreement negotiations.
  • 2018: Initiating the "Clean Oceans" campaign focused on marine conservation and reducing ocean plastic waste.
  • 2023: Launching the "Green Cities" project to promote green infrastructure and sustainable urban development in cities.

Today, Clean Earth continues to lead the charge in the fight against climate change and environmental degradation. Our commitment to direct action and collaboration with communities and governments has made us a leading force for environmental justice worldwide.
